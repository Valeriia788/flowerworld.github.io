ABOUT

Perhaps it is strange for some, but such a thing actually exists - flower therapy. Scientists have proven that aroma, appearance, color affect a person's mood and emotional state. Flowers can relax a person and adjust the mental state. When we receive a bouquet of flowers from someone close to us, we feel incredible happiness. And this feeling does not disappear for a long time. A bouquet of flowers can form a connection between people, express gratitude or other positive feelings. Working with the earth, transplanting pots fills a person with the energy of nature, makes one feel oneness with the forces of nature. And even simple contemplation can restore inner strength. The most common means of communication for people is language. Sometimes it is replaced by gestures, facial expressions, touch, and sometimes even a look is enough to express our feelings, intentions or wishes... Flowers are equally significant expressions of our attitude towards a dear person. Yes, they are the ones who speak when we are silent. It seems that the gentle petals and delicate aroma of each flower tells about everything that cannot even be expressed in words.

Flowers serve not only to decorate the room or as a beautiful gift. They also affect a person's health, mood, well-being. It's no secret that plants and flowers, including, give oxygen and absorb carbon dioxide. Many flowers contain healing properties, such as St. John's wort, primrose, chamomile. Indoor plants are able to regulate humidity in the room, purify the air, and kill bacteria. In addition, flower pots create a favorable atmosphere of coziness and comfort, whether at home or at the workplace. A bouquet of flowers for a birthday or on a simple day causes a wave of emotions that will be remembered for a long time.

1.Addressee's gender

Flowers are given to both girls and men, and for different sexes, their combinations differ greatly in appearance, structure and color. For a girl, a delicate bouquet of pink roses will be appropriate, and men's compositions usually include flowers of dark noble shades of blue, burgundy, red. There is also a difference in shape: women's can be rounded, while for men it is better to prefer elongated and sling shapes. The selection of types of flowers that can be presented to men should be approached thoroughly, but here, more individually, men are most often presented with roses, carnations, tulips, calla lilies, anthurium, gladiolus, orchids, lindens

2.An excuse for a bouquet

Wedding, anniversary, birthday, or maybe you want to please a loved one for no reason, for a certain situation you need a special bouquet. The bride's wedding bouquets are usually made in the shape of a ball, to simply congratulate friends on their marriage, optimally to make a large flower basket. An anniversary or a birthday requires celebration, but a certain modesty compared to a wedding, so bouquets are collected less conspicuously. However, these rules are quite conditional. At the celebration, it is allowed to present a miniature modest bouquet, just as you can give 101 roses as an expression of feelings for no reason. The main thing is that flowers bring joy.

3.Bouquet decor

The decor should complement the bouquet, and not overload it, so you should carefully consider a separate decorative element. The decor includes various ribbons, mesh, tinsel, sequins, miniature birds and insects are also used. For example, a pair of sunflowers made of wood will look good on the kraft wrapping paper of a bouquet. It is allowed to decorate the paper itself or other material for rotating flowers with stickers, decorative tape, and shaped elements from different materials. The main thing is that the decor should be appropriate, there should be no excess and it should match the composition in terms of style, color, and mood.
